Drinks

Raise your glass and enjoy our curated selection of fine beverages. Whether you're in the mood for a smooth glass of wine, a crisp, cold beer, the bold taste of whisky, or a flavorful liquor and soft drinks, we’ve got the perfect pour for every occasion. Sip, savor, and celebrate the moment.

Quality Food

Savor the taste of excellence with every bite. Our dishes are prepared with fresh ingredients, crafted with care, and served with passion. From classic favorites to signature creations, we deliver flavor that speaks for itself because quality is always on the menu.

Garden & Sports Bar Experience

Relax in our lush garden setting or catch all the live sports action in our vibrant sports bar. It’s the perfect space to unwind with friends, enjoy a cold drink, and soak in Kigali’s beautiful weather.

Conference Rooms & Event Space

Planning a business meeting, workshop, or private event? Our fully-equipped conference rooms accommodate up to 100 people, making us an ideal choice for corporate gatherings, seminars, and celebrations.

Online Order

Craving your favorites? Order online with ease and enjoy our delicious meals from the comfort of your home. Fast, simple, and convenient — your next bite is just a click away!

24/7 Service

Day or night, we’re here for you. Enjoy our top-quality food and drinks anytime with reliable, around-the-clock service. Because great taste never sleeps!
